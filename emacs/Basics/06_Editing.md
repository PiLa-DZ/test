### ============================================================
Copy Text
C-Space --> Region (Select Mode)
C-w     --> Kill (Cut Text)
M-w:    --> Copy
C-y     --> Past

CUA Mode --> Enable C-c and C-v to Copy and Past
    $>cua-mode

C-k: Kill. Deletes from the cursor to the end of the line.
### ============================================================
Undo Redo
C-_ --> Undo
C-? --> Redo

### ============================================================
C-s  : Search. press C-s again to find the next match.
